The Night Market of Magical Creatures

Just past the glowing river, I found a marketplace only seen by moonlight. Three magical beings offered me curious treasures…

The Dream Weaver

She sold bottled dreams. When I opened one, I saw floating cities and silver oceans — but only for a second.

The Time Tailor

He stitched seconds together like cloth. “Time is what you make,” he said, handing me a broken minute.

The Lantern Beast

A glowing animal with eyes like stars offered light from forgotten suns. I refused — too bright to understand.
